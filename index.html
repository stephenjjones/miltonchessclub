Milton Chess Club

Milton Chess Club is open to all members of Milton, Alpharetta, Roswell and surrounding communities.

Our focus is on introducing chess to kids. We have a homeschool group that will be meeting this fall at the Milton Library. Contact stephen@miltonchessclub.com for more information.

  • Homeschool Meetup

Weekly Homeschool Meetups at the Milton Library

We will be meeting this Fall at the Milton Library. The meetup is geared towards elementary-age kids, but is open to all. Chess is a great activity for a wide age-range of kids to enjoy together. The organizer is a homeschool dad of two (4&1/2 year old and 6&1/2 year old). Contact stephen@miltonchessclub.com for more information.
